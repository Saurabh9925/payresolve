Quick Answers

Common Questions

Do you guarantee payment recovery? +
No. We commit to a professional and ethical process, but cannot guarantee outcomes as recovery depends on the debtor's capacity. You pay nothing if we don't recover.
How much do you charge? +
Commission is 3%–7% of the recovered amount, agreed upfront. Absolutely no upfront fee. GST @ 18% applies on the commission amount.
How long does recovery take? +
Most cases resolve in 15–45 days. Timeline depends on debtor responsiveness. We keep you updated at every step.
Is my information confidential? +
Absolutely. We sign a formal NDA before you share any case details. All information is protected for 2 years post-engagement.
