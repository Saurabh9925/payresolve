Simple Process

How We Recover
Your Payments

A transparent, structured 4-step process designed to get results while preserving your business relationships.

01
Submit Your Case

Fill the online form or WhatsApp us with basic case details. Takes less than 5 minutes. No documents needed at this stage — just tell us what's happening.

What you need to share:
• Your name and business name
• Debtor name and approximate amount
• How long the payment has been pending
• Your contact number
02
Free Case Evaluation (24–48 hrs)

We review your case, sign an NDA, and then call you for a detailed discussion. We'll give you an honest assessment — including whether we think recovery is realistic. No obligation to proceed.

Documents to share after NDA:
• Copies of unpaid invoices
• Any signed agreements or purchase orders
• Debtor contact details
• Prior communication screenshots (if any)
03
Professional Recovery Process

Once you confirm to proceed and sign the Service Agreement, we initiate professional contact with the debtor. All communication is written, documented, and shared with you. We escalate strategically — starting soft, becoming firmer — while staying professional throughout.

WEEK 1
Professional first contact. Soft reminder. Understanding debtor's position.
WEEK 2–3
Follow-up escalation. Negotiation begins. Settlement terms explored.
WEEK 3–6
Final notices. Settlement finalised. Payment timeline agreed.
ONGOING
Regular updates to you. Strategy adjusted based on debtor response.
04
Recovery & Commission

Once the debtor pays you, our commission becomes payable within 7 working days. Our commission is calculated on the actual amount recovered. GST @ 18% applies on commission. Simple, transparent, fair.
